Beneath the dawn, the rivers sing,  
Whispers of hope on sparrow’s wing,  
Mountains rise with ancient grace,  
Guarding secrets time won’t erase.  

The stars ignite the velvet skies,  
Dreams awaken in weary eyes,  
Shadows fade where courage grows,  
Light returns as the morning shows.  

Every heartbeat writes its rhyme,  
Echoing love beyond all time.
